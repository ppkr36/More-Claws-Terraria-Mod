[h1]More Claws[/h1]

More Claws is a progression-based tModLoader mod that adds a new line of digging claws to Terraria.

Digging claws are designed as fast mining tools with reduced range. They are meant to be useful for mining and exploration, while regular pickaxes still remain better for building and precise block work.
