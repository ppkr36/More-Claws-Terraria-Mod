More Claws adds a new progression-based line of digging claws to Terraria.

Digging claws are fast mining tools with reduced range, giving players an alternative to standard pickaxes without fully replacing them.

Current content:
- Copper Claws
- Starting item support

Planned content:
- Full pre-Hardmode claw progression
- Hardmode claw progression
- Hallowed Claws
- Zenith Claws
